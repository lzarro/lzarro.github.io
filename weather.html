Infared Light

 Because the black hole is so far away from earth, and invisible to the human eye, scientists choose to try to detect infrared light from the black hole. Being a infrared light focused telescope, James Webb Space Telescope is able to reach further deep into space than habbo space telescope due to it being capable to record longer wavelength coverage.
